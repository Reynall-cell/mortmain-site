From the forge: directional combat

Patch 3 · VI · MMXXVI
✠

The first pillar stands. Directional strikes are in: choose where the blow comes from, and your opponent must read it before it lands. Stamina governs everything — swing wild and the sun will set on you early.

What works on the bench today: directional attack chains, stamina costs for light and heavy actions, and passive directional blocking. The duel already feels like what we promised — a conversation in steel.

Next on the anvil: animation polish and the block-parry boundary. We will ring the bell again when there is something worth showing.
