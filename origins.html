Who walks the road

Twenty lives before the plague. Choose who you were — it decides how you survive, what you owe, and what you hear in the dark. No gift makes you stronger against another man: strength lives in iron and hands.

An early design. Gifts and burdens are named in words, not numbers — the numbers will come with the game, and will change more than once.
